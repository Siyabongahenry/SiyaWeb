Project Management

As an engineering student, I always had a pile of projects to complete with different groups, therefore, it was compulsory to follow a specific project management style to be able to manage the work. The main challenge with project management was the management itself, it was never easy to keep up with the work schedule but we eventually managed.

Experience Summary

  • Project proporsal
  • Main Project
  • Presentation
  • Final Report

Experience

The most valuable lesson I learnt in project management is the value of time. At the beginning you might get tempted into thinking that you will have enough time to complete the project and procrastinate. The only thing that procrastination does, is to make your work a pile that you will have to complete in less time under pressure. This will not only result to burn out but to complete failure of the project.

When working with groups, fairly distribution of the work is mandatory. Failure to do so will result in mismanagement of the projet which will lead to lagging in some portion of the project. This process can only be accomplished by choosing a strong group leader.

One of the prominent tool I learnt in project management is a Gantt Chart. This tool makes it easier to manage your time and also track your progress. Using it has help me in making sure that I complete all my projects on time.

Experience Summary

Experience

The most interesting reports I ever wrote were for my electronic modules. We use to compare two or more eletronic devices that has the same functionaities, but manufactured by different companies. The aim of these reports was to find the best perfoming device in the list. This was done by collecting enough data about the components of the devices and compare them. For instance, we will compare two or more smartphones that are competing in the current year to find the best option.

In physics we use to write logbooks first before finalizing the reports. The logbooks were used for collection data in the labs, it had full explanation of how an experiment was conducted and all the oberved data. The reports were a summary of what was in the logbooks. These reports used be interesting if you clearly undestand the theory behind the experiment you are conducting.

We also use to write report after completing a certain projects or workshop. If it was a project, we would write about the challenges we faced while working on projects, and what should be done in future to improve the projects. As for workshops, we used to write a brief reports about our experience at the workshop.
