Feedback

The most valuable lesson I learnt in project management is the value of time. Once you start with the project, time flies. Therefore, you should always keep track of the project. Using tools like Gantt Chart helps a lot.

When working with projects, you're most likely to work in large groups. Thus, communication is significant and plays a large role in the success of the project. Having good communication skills is essential, it does not only benefit you but the group as whole.

Writing Report

I acquired a lot about reports in Physics practicals. I started writing a pile of report in my second year. We use to write logbooks while doing practicals and compile them into a reports after some time.

Steps toward a report

  • Research
  • Data Collection
  • Logbook
  • Report

My Experience

A reports is just a summary of what you have done, this could be through practical means or observations. I use to hate writing reports but with no choise I had to give them fake love until it is real. Thus, here I am today not getting stressed out, when I get a chance to write a report.

In physics we use to write logbooks first before finalizing the reports. The logbooks were used to collect data in the labs, it had full explanation of how an experiment was conducted and all the oberved data. These reports used be interesting if you clearly undestand the theory behind the experiment you are conducting.

We also use to write report after completing certain projects or workshop. If it was a project, we would write about the challenges we faced while working on the projects, and what should be done in future to improve the projects. As for workshops, we used to write a brief reports about our experience at the workshop.

The most interesting reports I've ever wrote were for my electronics modules. In these reports we use to compare current electronic devices that are already in a competition. Our aim was to always find the best beforming device in the list.
